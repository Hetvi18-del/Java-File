How DocuChain Works

Simple steps to secure your documents forever

1

Upload Document

Upload your document and we'll create a unique cryptographic hash that represents your file.

2

Blockchain Registration

The document hash is registered on our blockchain with a timestamp and your digital signature.

3

Get Verification ID

Receive a unique verification ID that anyone can use to confirm your document's authenticity.

4

Share & Verify

Share your verification ID with anyone who needs to confirm your document is genuine.
